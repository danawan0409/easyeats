Tofu and Noodles Recipe

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for frying)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons green onions, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 small red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Drain and press the tofu to remove excess water, then cut into cubes.
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the tofu and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add the garlic, ginger, carrots, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 5 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Mix in the soy sauce, sesame oil, and hoisin sauce (if using). Toss in the cooked noodles and stir to coat.
  6. Top with green onions, sesame seeds, lime juice, and cilantro. Serve immediately.
